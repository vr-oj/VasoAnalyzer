Scope audit inventory for V3.0. Decisions are grounded in the stated direction: pressure myography analysis + visualization + export + GIF.

Legend: KEEP = retain and harden. DEPRECATE = remove after staged withdrawal. REPLACE = retire in favor of a simpler/cleaner V3 feature. MERGE = consolidate into core or another subsystem.
